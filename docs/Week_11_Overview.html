Fixed Effects Lab

Overview

This lab in Week 11 introduces Fixed Effects models, a foundational strategy for controlling unobserved, stable differences across units in panel data. You’ll estimate FE models in Stata and learn how to interpret within-unit effects over time — while reinforcing why this improves causal inference relative to cross-sectional regression.

Learning Objectives

At the end of this week, you will be able to:

  • Structure panel data properly in Stata (xtset)
  • Estimate fixed effects models using xtreg
  • Interpret within-unit effects cleanly and carefully
  • Explain what FE does — and does not — solve
